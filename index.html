About Baier International

BAIER INTERNATIONAL is a multi-sector private family office led by Founder Dalton T. Baier. Our mandate is to acquire, build, and steward generational assets across aviation, global finance, natural resources, agriculture, entertainment, and sovereign-tier capital structures.

We combine the discretion of old-world banking dynasties with the modern execution speed of elite private capital.
